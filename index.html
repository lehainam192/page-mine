What it does

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

PageMine gives you precise, non-destructive control over every page you visit. Elements are hidden, not deleted — you can restore anything anytime.

🎯
Click-to-hide selection

Enter selection mode and hover over any element. A live blue outline shows what you're targeting. One click hides it instantly.

💾
Auto-save per site

Your hidden elements are automatically remembered per domain. Visit YouTube, Reddit, or any site again — your customizations are already applied.

↩️
Restore anything, anytime

All hidden elements are listed in the popup. Click × next to any item to bring it back. Nothing is deleted — everything is reversible.

🗂️
Sites dashboard

See all websites where you've hidden elements in one overview. Jump to a site, reset individual sites, or manage your full configuration.

📤
Export & import settings

Back up your entire PageMine configuration to a JSON file. Import it on another machine. Your distraction-free web, anywhere you go.

⚡
Instant toggle on/off

Need to see hidden elements temporarily? One click toggles PageMine on or off for the current tab — no need to unhide or reset anything.

How it works

Four clicks to a cleaner web

No tutorials, no learning curve. If you can click, you can use PageMine.

1
Click the icon

Open PageMine from your Chrome toolbar to see the popup for your current site.

2
Start selecting

Hit "Start Selecting" — your cursor becomes a precision tool for picking elements.

3
Click to hide

Hover over any element to preview it, then click to make it disappear immediately.

4
It stays gone

Settings are saved instantly. Refresh, close, come back later — it's still hidden.
